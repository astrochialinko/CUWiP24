Talk & Poster Guidelines

If you have submitted a student oral presentation/ poster presentation request during your registration, you will receive detailed information in a separate email. The student poster sessions will take place Saturday, Jan 22, 17:15 – 18:15.

Poster set up: Saturday, January 22, Morning Coffee Break.
Take down: Sunday, January 23, Morning Coffee Break.

We will have judges for the posters and talks. The “Best Poster Award” and “Best Talk Award” will be announced in the concluding session on Sunday, January 23.
